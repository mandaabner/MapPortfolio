Map Portfolio

Static and interative web maps

Big Bend National Park

This interactive map includes popups showing various amenities and points of interest at Big Bend National Park. This map was created using Leaflet.

East Pinnacle

This Mapbox map includes a raster elevation layer that was converted using GDAl and QGIS 3.2. PostGIS was used to store and query the national dataset.

Appalachian Trail

A historic map shows Maryland, West Virginia, and Virginia with the Appalachian Trail highlighted in red. Data sources include Appalachian Trail Conservancy and Natural Earth. and Erwin Raisz map georeferenced in QGIS 3.2.1 using GDAL Georeferencer.

Protected Areas of Australia

A static map of National Parks and other protected areas in Australia. The map was created with QGIS 3.2.1 and data sources include Protected Planet and Natural Earth.

Maryland Towns

Three Maryland towns with popups showing populations of each. Map created using Leaflet and OpenStreetMap.

Route Home

A commuter route through Lexington, KY with popups for places of interest. The map was created using Leaflet, CartoDB, and OpenStreetMap.

About me

Hi, I'm Amanda. I spend my days fighting gum disease with curets and lasers. In my spare time I create interactive web maps with New Maps Plus.

I graduated from Eastern Kentucky University with a B.A. in Sociology and a minor in International Studies. I spent 3.5 years in a Camphill community outside of Philadelphia as an AmeriCorps member. I currently practice as a registered dental hygienist as well as teach periodontal instrumentation at the University of Kentucky College of Dentistry.
